About Us

About US

The Eti-Osa Youth Organisation is the apex and unifying group of youths in Eti-Osa, comprising members from all communities. It is a non-partisan and forward-thinking organisation dedicated to the creationof a unified Eti-Osa and a shared future.
It is also the formidable voice that seeks to promote and safeguard the interests of justice, fairness, equity, and equality in Eti-Osa while guaranteeing that we are unified on all frontiers at all times, regardless of the prevailing circumstances.
The Organisation arose from the need for us (the youths of Eti-Osa) to have an all-youth platform that will uniformly magnify our voices on subjects pertaining to the promotion and protection of our welfare,as well as other problems of major importance as they pertain to Eti-Osa.

Our Core Objective

• To promote community involvement in addressing and identifying youth issues to achieve the Organization's goals.
• To involve youths in activities that encourage self- determination and progressiveness for the youth and further enhance the skills of those involved;
• To provide a safe, supportive, fun, caring, and confidential environment conducive to learning and positive change.
• To establish a platform that facilitates collaboration, knowledge sharing, and unity among youth-driven organisations, Unions, Associations, and Societies in Eti-Osa;
• To bridge the gap between Eti-Osa youths and other pertinent bodies;
• To promote sustainable livelihoods for communities through outreach, mentoring, research, vocational education and training,and life skills training programmes;
• To create strong bonds with groups that share our objectives to give the youths more opportunitiesto develop their skills and aptitudes for long-term success in their endeavour as well as for the development of their communities;
• To garner public and governmental support for Eti-Osa

Our History

The Eti-Osa Youth Organization (EYO) is the apex and unifying umbrella body for youth across Eti-Osa. The Organization enjoys goodwill and bank on endorsements from the relevant bodies across the Eti-Osa which we mutually collaborate with to foster our founding credo.
The Organization which espouses nationalism virtues within the societal space and beyond, harps on the need for collaborative and collective efforts amongst Eti-Osa indigene and residents of goodwill to unify the local government beyond preexisting divisions, and excellently mould the next generation of thought leaders who will ensure the entrenchment of equality, justice and fairness in the local government; and also ensure optimal utilization of Eti-Osa’s unquantifiable human and natural endowments for the developmental projection of the local government within the national space.
The Organization was birthed from a strong desire by its founding members championed by Olasupo Jubril Adedimeji (former National President of the Eti-Osa Indigenous Students’ Association) and encouraged by Hon. Abdulmumin Edidi (Member of the Nigerian Youth Parliament, Representing the Lagos Central Senatorial District) to create fusion and a unifying platform for Eti-Osa youths to productively interact on issues that orbit around the development of youth to reach their full potential, utilization of Eti-Osa resources for the betterment of Eti-Osa, inculcation of peace and unity in our respective communities, assuaging the practical and functional identified deficiencies that hinders the progress of Eti-Osa and her youths, and the imbibing of contemporary developmental skills in view of the world’s realities.
With the letters for a clarion call for a unified Eti-Osa sent out by Olasupo Jubril Adedimeji to all Eti-Osa youth leaders/representatives across Eti-Osa and the several virtual meetings held, the Eti-Osa Youth Organization (EYO) was formed which subsequently resulted to the constitution of the ‘EYO Round Table Discussion Committee’ led by Comr. Sodic Ademola Balogun to hold the first physical meeting of youth leaders/representative across Eti-Osa. This round table discussion further consequently brought into the existence of the creation of the EYO Ad-hoc Committees by her founding members.
Upon the creation of the EYO Ad-hoc committees, the committees promptly hit the ground running which thereafter resulted to the construction of the organization core structure as a result of the rigorous and unrelenting effort of all members of the Ad-hoc committees. Since the impact of the EYO Ad-hoc committees, EYO has continued to strongly pursued its founding aims by widening its membership with the acceptance of new communities and youth driven Organizations/Unions/Associations/Societies across Eti-Osa for the good of Eti-Osa.
