Who we are

The Eti-Osa Youth Organization (EYO) is the apex and unifying body of youths with membership across all communities in Eti-Osa that serves as an advisory, assistance and representation body for youth in Eti-Osa with the purpose of organizing to address its singularity and complexity; promoting unity, education and democratic principles, encouraging the participation and development of youth in Eti-Osa; and undertaking actions to build more just and equitable society for the good of Eti-Osa.

Membership

Full Membership

A full member of the Organization is a person who is an indigene or resident of Eti-Osa up to 18 to 35 years (inclusive) of age and associated to any community based youth group in Eti-Osa or any youth organization in Eti-Osa recognized by EYO.

Honorary Membership

A honorary membership status is granted to an adherent who is an indigene or resident of Eti-Osa who, although, not associated with members from EYO, but support the organization or want to develop it

Core Objectives

The Organization’s Core Objectives Include:

Education

Promoting education and raising awareness
about important issues affecting young people.

Unity

To unite the youths in Eti-Osa and coordinate
their activities.

Justice

To pursue justice for youths in Eti-Osa in view of the
rampant injustice in the society.

Network and Collaborations

Facilitating networking opportunities and
fostering collaboration among the youth.

Good Citizenship

Promoting Unity and Patriotism.

Representation

To represent the interests of Eti-Osa youths in
all National and International matters

EYO ORGANIZES ETI-OSA YOUTH STAKEHOLDERS ROUNDTABLE DISCUSSION

On the 24th day of September, 2023, the Eti-Osa Youth Organization organized a roundtable discussion at Osapa, Eti-Osa to chart a course for the progress and development of youths across the Eti-Osa Local Government Area.

During this round table discussion, solutions to problems that affect the youths such as deprivation of opportunities, insecurity, and unemployment, amongst other problems were adequately proffered to chart a course for the progress and development of Eti-Osa.
