interested in joining us?

In EYO, there are two categories of membership

Honorary Memberships

1

A provisional membership status is granted to an adherent who is an indigene or resident of Eti-Osa who, although, not associated with members from EYO, but support the organization or want to develop it.

Full Membership

2

A full member of the Organization is a person who is an indigene or resident of Eti-Osa up to 18 to 35 years (inclusive) of age and associated to any community based youth group in Eti-Osa or any youth organization in Eti-Osa recognized by EYO.
